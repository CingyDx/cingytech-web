Rules

Read the world, not the web.

Goal

You see a Street View style location and must identify the country from visual clues: signs, language, road markings, driving side, plates, domains, flags, architecture, climate and vegetation.

Solo mode

You have 3 minutes per round. Guess the country. Correct guesses add to your streak; wrong guesses or timeout end the run. Results show correct country, your guess and distance.

Duel mode

Two online players share the same location. Both start with 6000 HP and get one guess. When one player guesses, the opponent has 15 seconds. The farther player takes damage based on distance difference. From round 5 the multiplier rises by 0.5x per round. Duel ends at 0 HP.

Fair play

No Google search, translators, external maps or looking up visible names outside the game. Play only from what the scene gives you.
