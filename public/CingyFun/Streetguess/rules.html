Rules

Guess where the Street View dropped you.

Use only what you can see in the panorama: signs, languages, road markings, cars, plates, flags, buildings, landscape, domains, climate and vegetation.

Solo Country Streak

  • You get one real Google Street View location each round.
  • You have 3 minutes by default.
  • Click the real Google map to place or drag a pin.
  • Your distance creates a score from 0 to 5000.
  • The country must match to keep the streak alive.
  • Wrong country or timer expiry resets the streak.

Online 1v1 Duel

  • Both players start with 6000 HP.
  • Both players join the same room code with separate `/CingyFun` accounts.
  • Both players get the same real Street View location.
  • Whoever guesses first starts the 15 second pressure timer for the opponent.
  • If the opponent has a pin when the timer expires, the pin is submitted automatically.
  • The farther player takes damage based on distance difference.
  • Round 1-4 use 1x damage. Round 5 is 1.5x, then +0.5x every round.
  • The duel ends when one player reaches 0 HP.

Fair play

No outside help.

  • No Google searching city names, roads or signs.
  • No translator.
  • No external maps.
  • No image search.
  • Play by visual clues only.
